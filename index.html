the @australia project

@australia is a shared commons of data and tools for Australia. Made by the public, used by the public.

Featuring work from people with the OpenAustralia Foundation, Taskforce.is, and the Internet.

Help out:

  • fix open issues or share new ideas
  • maybe scrape a website
  • start a project of your own
  • use our stuff! (and tell us about it)

More about why we're doing this.

More projects at work over at github.com/australia.
